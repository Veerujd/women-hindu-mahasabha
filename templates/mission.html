Mission & Objectives

Dedicated to the service of the Veerashaiva Lingayath community through education, culture, and unity.

OUR VISION

A United and Progressive Community

To establish a global network of empowered Veerashaiva Lingayaths who are rooted in their spiritual heritage while leading in professional, social, and economic spheres.

  • Cultural Excellence
  • Universal Unity
  • Ethical Leadership
OUR MISSION

Empowering Generations through Heritage

To provide sustainable support systems for education, preserve ancient spiritual teachings (Vachanas), and build infrastructure that fosters community growth and social harmony.

  • Educational Support
  • Spiritual Awareness
  • Social Justice

Core Objectives

📚

Educational Welfare

Establishing scholarship funds and educational institutions to support bright minds from our community.

🏛️

Cultural Heritage

Documenting and preserving the 12th-century Vachana literature and the teachings of Lord Basavanna.

🌍

Social Unity

Creating a platform for Veerashaivas worldwide to connect, collaborate, and support each other.

⚖️

Legal & Civic Rights

Protecting the rights and interests of the community at the state and national levels.

🏥

Medical & Social Services

Providing healthcare support and organizing blood donation camps and social welfare activities.

💎

Economic Development

Encouraging entrepreneurship and providing vocational training to empower the youth economically.
